Product Warranty

Our products are backed by warranty to ensure long-term performance, reliability, and customer peace of mind.

Warranty Overview

RS Window Films provides warranty coverage on selected products against manufacturing defects and performance issues, subject to proper usage and maintenance. Our warranty reflects our commitment to quality and professional installation standards.

What Is Covered

  • ✔ Air bubbles caused by adhesive failure
  • ✔ Peeling or cracking under normal conditions
  • ✔ Discoloration or fading (as per product specification)
  • ✔ UV & IR performance degradation within warranty limits

What Is Not Covered

  • ✖ Damage caused by accidents or external impact
  • ✖ Improper cleaning or use of abrasive chemicals
  • ✖ Unauthorized removal or reinstallation
  • ✖ Natural wear beyond warranty period
