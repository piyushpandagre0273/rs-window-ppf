Product Warranty

Our products are backed by warranty to ensure long-term performance, reliability, and customer peace of mind.

Warranty Overview

RS provides warranty coverage on selected products against manufacturing defects and performance issues, subject to proper usage and maintenance. Our warranty reflects our commitment to quality and professional installation standards. Every product is tested to ensure it meets our rigorous quality standards before reaching you.

What Is Covered

✓

Air Bubbles

Caused by adhesive failure under normal conditions

✓

Peeling or Cracking

Under normal usage and weather conditions

✓

Discoloration

Fading or color changes beyond product specification

✓

Performance Degradation

UV & IR rejection performance drop within warranty limits

What Is Not Covered

✕

Accident Damage

Damage from accidents, collisions, or external impact

✕

Improper Care

Improper cleaning or use of abrasive chemicals

✕

Unauthorized Work

Unauthorized removal or reinstallation by non-professionals

✕

Natural Wear

Normal wear beyond the warranty period

Warranty Claim Process ⌄

Step 1: Contact Us

Reach out to our support team with your invoice and product details

Step 2: Inspection

Step 3: Resolution

Product Care & Maintenance ⌄

• Wait 48 hours after installation before cleaning

• Use only soft microfiber cloths for cleaning

• Avoid harsh chemicals—use mild soap and water only

• Never use squeegees or sharp objects on treated windows

• Regular maintenance extends product lifespan and maintains warranty validity

Warranty Exclusions & Limitations ⌄

• Labor costs for removal or reinstallation are not covered

• Damage from vehicle accidents or natural disasters is excluded

• Warranty is limited to product replacement or repair, not refunds

• RS is not liable for indirect or consequential damages

• All warranty claims must be filed within 30 days of issue discovery
