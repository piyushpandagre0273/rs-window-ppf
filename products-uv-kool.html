What Makes UV Kool Different?

UV400 Technology – Beyond Standard Films

Unlike conventional window films, UV Kool features a high-density nano-ceramic core bonded with a UV400 protective layer. This advanced architecture blocks harmful rays up to 400 nanometers, including violet light that most films fail to stop—ensuring unmatched protection for passengers and interiors.
